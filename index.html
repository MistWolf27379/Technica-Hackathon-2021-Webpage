About

It is recorded that 60% of the patients in the US tend to forget to take their meds.

This is a well documented and a longstanding problem called Medical Non-Adherence.

To counter this problem, Team ASSK has developed a Medicine Dispensing Bot, which will assist patients in taking their medicine in time.

Features

Fully Automated

The bot is fully automated, it requires minimal human intervention to deliver medicine to patients

Time Regulated

The bot is regulated by time, and hence brings the medicine to patients on time

Medicine Alarm

The bot has a sound module, which notifies the patients about their medicine taking time.

Easy to Use

The bot is easy to use, the patient just needs to pick their medicine from the container
